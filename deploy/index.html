COLLABORATION

Lady M's collectible and limited edition 2020 mooncake lantern is a unique collaboration with Netflix and Pearl Studio for Over The Moon, an animation film featuring the story of a young girl's journey to meet Chang'e, the legendary Moon Goddess.

When she was young, Fei Fei's mother and father would tell her the story of Chang'e. Years later, after her mother's death, her father is finally moving on and is ready to get remarried.

Fei Fei is having a tough time adjusting to the idea of a new mother and stepbrother. She decides to build a rocket ship to head to the moon in search of the goddess, Chang'e, her mother told her stories about.

LIMITED EDITION

Lady M's lantern includes mooncakes made in collaboration with Kee Wah Bakery. Sweet egg custard mooncakes feature a stamp of the classic Lady M logo. Chocolate custard mooncakes are stamped with the mythical moon rabbit. Each lantern includes three pieces of each flavor, six mini mooncakes in total.

This interactive mooncake lantern showcases a light-up feature that highlights the gold and jade green lantern design. Click a button to activate the lights and enjoy! Slide open the lantern to unveil a collection of mini mooncakes. The mooncake lantern is a special gift that is both entertaining and delicious.
